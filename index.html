About Us

ESHAA APPARELS was founded in October 2009 by Mrs. Lathalakshmi Dhinakaran with a mission to make world-class garments and be perceived as the best in the garment industry. In May 2010, the company transformed into a Partnership Firm represented by its partners viz. Mr.V. Dhinakaran as Managing Director and Mr. M. Raju, Mr.K. Mohan Kumar, Mr.R. Miller, Mrs.R. Nagavithya as board of Directors.
